Subject objectives and learning outcomes

The objectives of the Fundamentals of Computer Engineering course are to introduce students to the foundational concepts of computer systems and their components. It aims to provide a clear understanding of both hardware and software principles, including their interaction and functionality. The course emphasizes developing problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and programming abilities to prepare students for practical applications in engineering. The course also highlights emerging technologies like artificial intelligence and embedded systems to expose students to modern advancements in the field. By the end of the course, we will be able to demonstrate an understanding of computer engineering principles and apply them to solve real-world problems. Furthermore, we will develop the ability to evaluate new technologies and collaborate effectively in engineering projects, as well as being prepared for advanced studies or careers in computer engineering.
