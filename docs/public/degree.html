mathematical and computer engineering

Our degree in mathematical and computer engineering combines advanced mathematics, programming, and problem-solving to design and improve systems, software, and technologies. It focuses on using math to create models, analyze data, and develop efficient solutions, while also teaching how computers and networks work, including hardware design, coding, and cybersecurity. As graduates we can pursue careers as data analysts, software developers, network engineers, cybersecurity specialists, and researchers in industries like finance, technology, robotics, and artificial intelligence.
